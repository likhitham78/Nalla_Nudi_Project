📘 Nalla-Nudi
Android App for Bridging Technical English Vocabulary with Kannada
🌍 Project Overview

Nalla-Nudi is an Android application designed as a bridge dictionary for technical terms.
It helps Kannada-medium students understand English technical vocabulary in a simple, contextual, and native-language-friendly way.

The app is built to solve a major learning gap where students understand concepts but struggle with English technical terminology in higher education.

🎯 Problem Statement

Students from Kannada-medium schools face difficulty when transitioning to English in higher studies, especially in STEM fields.

While they understand concepts, they struggle with:

Technical vocabulary (e.g., Photosynthesis, Trigonometry, Gravity)
Lack of subject-specific dictionaries
Absence of contextual meaning in native language

👉 Standard dictionaries are too general and not education-focused.

💡 Vision

Nalla-Nudi acts as a confidence-building educational tool that:

Translates technical English terms into simple Kannada
Provides contextual explanations with examples
Helps students transition smoothly into higher education

It is not just a dictionary — it is a learning bridge.
